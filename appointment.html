How to book an appointment

Booking an appointment with UK HealthXpress is a simple and quick process. Your GP or consultant issues a blood form which provides us with information of what you need to be tested for. Once you acquire this form you can then contact us directly to arrange appointment at a convenient time and place for yourself.

If booking a blood draw service away from the clinic please keep in mind your samples will be collected at your specified location and delivered to your local GP or laboratory for testing, as a result you will not recieve the blood test results until a later date.

Our out-of-clinic blood draw services are affordable and our fee will be discussed with you before you arrange an appointment.

    Cancelling/rearranging an appointment

    If you cannot make it to an appointment, please phone us and let us know in advance so that we can either cancel or rearrange the appointment to a later date.
