Blood and Urine testing

At UK HealthXpress we offer a variety of blood testing/withdrawal services to suit you and make your experience as comfortable as possible.

  • Blood test at the clinic

    • We provide daily appointments at our clinic, visit us and we can test your blood on the day!

  • Blood withdrawal at your home

    • For our patients who are unable to come visit us at the surgery, we have mobile phlebotomists at hand who you can book an appointment with to collect your blood from the comfort of your own home.

  • Blood withdrawal at the workplace

    • Too busy to visit the surgery? At UK HealthXpress we understand that you may get tied up at work alot of the time, as a result we've made it possible for you to book an appointment with our mobile phlebotomists to withdraw blood from you at a time suitable to you in your work place.

  • Pediatric blood tests

    • You can book a blood test for your children and we can guarantee you that our phlebotomists will provide a pain-free and enjoyable experience.

  • Urine tests

    • We also handle urine testing at the surgery. You can pickup a urine sample bottle from the surgery and hand it in whenever you can. Or have it delivered to and picked from your home or office.
